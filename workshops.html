Workshops

Purpose: I have been conducting workshops during semester breaks to augment conventional curriculum based learning by exposition of essential technical topics keeping an emphasis on project development with "learning by doing" approach.

Salient Features:
1. Programming rigor: Solving problems by writing programs.
2. Content: Latest topics not covered in regular curriculum.
3. Practical hands-on: Emphasize on project development.
4. Delivery: Head start introduction through a tutorial style exposition of concepts.

Target Audience: Students in their B.Tech./M.C.A./M.Tech. programs.

Duration: Conducted mostly during the Summer Vacation, mostly in the months of June - July, students are expected to attend regular for initial few days, thereafter, they are allowed to develop projects remotely.
