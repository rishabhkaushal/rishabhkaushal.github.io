I initially joined the institution in 2011 when it was known as the Indira Gandhi Institute of Technology (a constituent college of GGSIP University).

From July 2023 to June 2025, I worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Institute of Data Science, Department of Advanced Computing Sciences, Maastricht University, Netherlands.
