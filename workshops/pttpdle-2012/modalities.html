The Workshop on Programming Tools and Techniques for Project Development in Linux Environment shall be conducted in view of following modalities:


    * Workshop shall operate under the aegis of  IGIT ACM Student Chapter and in a selffinance mode as far as financial support is concerned.

    * To meet the expenses of the Workshop (which includes Honorarium to Resource Persons, Workshop Participant’s Stationary Kit, Refreshments, Inauguration and Valedictory Ceremonies  and any other Miscellaneous), a nominal registration fee shall be charged.

    * Workshop venue shall be IGIT. Lab sessions shall be held in Computer Center, IGIT
