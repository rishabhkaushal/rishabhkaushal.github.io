Modalities of the 3rd Summer Workshop on “ Secure Web-based Application Development” are:

1. Resource Persons: Workshop would be conducted by in-house faculty at Department of Information Technology.

2. Logistics: Computer Center / Software Design Lab with around 40 computers shall be used for practical sessions. Participants are encouraged to bring their laptops.

3. Software: All necessary software shall be provided for both Linux and Windows environment during the Workshop and instructions for its installation shall be given during the Workshop so that students are able to start building toy web applications right away.

4. Pre-requisite: Workshop assumes no prior knowledge of web technologies and security. Familiarity of any one programming language (C or C++) is expected.

5. Certification: Each participating student shall receive certificates after successful completion of the Workshop.
