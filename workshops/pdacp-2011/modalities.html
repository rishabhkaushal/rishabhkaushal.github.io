The Workshop on “Project Development using Advanced C Programming” shall be conducted in view of following modalities:

1. Resource Persons: Workshop would be conducted by in-house faculty at Department of Information Technology.

2. Registration Fee: A nominal registration fee of Rs.1500/- (one thousand and five hundred only) is to be charged from the participating students.

3. Support: Workshop is being organized on self-financing basis, the revenue

generated from the Registration Fee shall be used for expenses incurred during the Workshop.

4. Logistics: Networked Lab with around 30 computers, projector and whiteboard.

5. Guest Lectures: Representatives with proven record from academia and industry would be invited to orient students towards real world challenges facing the industry and how academicians are helping to deal with them.

6. Certification: Each participating student shall receive certificates after successful completion of the Workshop.

7. Automated Evaluation: An online web based system developed in-house would be used for evaluation. The system is designed to automatically compile and test programming problems uploaded by students, thereby enabling effective and speedy assessment of students’ progress.

8. Refreshments: Participating students shall be provided refreshments twice a day in forenoon and afternoon sessions to keep them refreshed physically.

           
            9. Lab Sessions: Intensive lab work is planned each day to give a hands-on approach to the concepts discussed in theory sessions.
